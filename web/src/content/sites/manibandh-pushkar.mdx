overview: |
  Manibandh Shaktipeeth, on the Gayatri Hills near Pushkar, Rajasthan,
  also known as Manivedika Shaktipeeth, is one of the classical 51
  Shakti Peethas, traditionally marking the spot where Sati's wrists
  fell. The goddess is worshipped here as Gayatri, the personification of
  the Vedic Gayatri Mantra and revered as the Vedic Mother, with Shiva
  present as Sarvanand.
