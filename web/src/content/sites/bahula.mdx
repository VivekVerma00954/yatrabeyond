  Bahula Temple, in Purba Bardhaman district, West Bengal, is one of the
  classical 51 Shakti Peethas. The Peethanirnaya and Shivacharita texts
  identify this as the site where the left arm (bahu) of Sati fell
  during Shiva's grief-stricken Rudra Tandava, giving the goddess her
  name, Bahula.
