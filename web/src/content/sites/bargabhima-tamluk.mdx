overview: |
  Bargabhima Temple, in Tamluk, Purba Medinipur district, West Bengal,
  also known as Vibhash Shakti Peetha and Bhimakali Mandir, is one of
  the classical 51 Shakti Peethas, traditionally marking the spot where
  the ankle of Sati's left leg fell. The goddess is worshipped here as
  Kapalini, also known locally as Bargabhima Devi.

history: |
  The temple is traditionally dated to roughly 1,150 years old, built by
  a Maharaja of the Mayor dynasty, making it one of the more
  historically deep-rooted Shakti Peethas within West Bengal's dense
  regional cluster.
