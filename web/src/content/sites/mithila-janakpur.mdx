overview: |
  Mithila Shakti Peetha, also known as Uma Devi Temple, in Janakpur,
  Nepal, near the Bihar border, is one of the classical 51 Shakti
  Peethas, traditionally marking the spot where Sati's left shoulder
  fell. The goddess is worshipped here as Uma (a form of Parvati), with
  Shiva present as Mahodar. The temple's white, fort-like architecture
  houses the deity images on a mountain rock.
