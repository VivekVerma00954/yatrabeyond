overview: |
  Alopi Devi Temple, in Alopibagh, Prayagraj, near the Sangam where the
  Ganga, Yamuna, and legendary Sarasvati meet, is one of the classical
  51 Shakti Peethas, traditionally marking the spot where Sati's
  fingers, or in some tellings her right hand, fell and disappeared,
  giving the goddess her name "Alopi" (the one who disappeared/vanished).
  This platform notes that some source lists instead name a separate,
  nearby "Lalita Devi Temple" in Prayagraj as the true Shakti Peetha
  site; both temples exist and are genuinely distinct, and this platform
  flags rather than resolves the naming ambiguity between them.
