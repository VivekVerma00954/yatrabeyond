overview: |
  Bhabanipur Shakti Peetha, also known as Aparna Devi Temple, near
  Sherpur, Bogra district, Bangladesh, is one of the classical 51 Shakti
  Peethas and one of seven such sites within present-day Bangladesh.
  Body-part attribution is genuinely unsettled across sources: the most
  commonly cited claim is Sati's left anklet, though other sources
  instead cite her right eye or the ribs of the left side of her chest.
  This platform notes all three claims rather than asserting one as
  definitively settled. The goddess is worshipped as Aparna, with Shiva
  present as Vaman.
